Key Characters

Magic-Infused Dialects

In the Echotongue universe, language is power. Each planet has developed its own dialect infused with magical properties. Those who can master these languages can manipulate reality, control elements, and even alter minds.

Lydia Nightshade possesses a rare gift: the ability to speak all thirty-two dialects. This makes her both invaluable and dangerous to the powers that rule the universe.

Story Timeline

Echotongue follows Lydia Nightshade's journey across the thirty-two planets as she uncovers the truth about her past, her unique abilities, and the dark secrets of the Universal Council.

About the Author

S.E. Blackwood is an award-winning speculative fiction author known for intricate world-building and complex characters. With a background in linguistics and mythology, Blackwood creates universes where language itself becomes a source of power and danger.

Echotongue is the culmination of seven years of research and writing, drawing inspiration from diverse mythologies, language structures, and the eternal human quest for truth in the face of overwhelming power.
