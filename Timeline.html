ECHOTONGUE Timeline

Follow the journey through key events without spoilers. This timeline only covers what's revealed in the story preview.

SPOILER WARNING

This timeline contains only events from the preview pages (1-212). No plot twists, outcomes, or major revelations beyond the initial chapters are shown.

PART ONE: Beginnings

Lydia's Life in Riverside

Lydia Nightshade lives with her aunt and uncle, feeling increasingly invisible after the birth of her cousin May. She attends Riverside Academy where she faces challenges both at home and school.

A seemingly ordinary life with hidden depths

The School Incident

A confrontation with teachers leads to unexpected consequences at Riverside Academy, forcing Lydia to discover abilities she never knew she had.

Sometimes the truth reveals itself in flames

The Forest Journey

Lydia's uncle takes her on a trip to the forest she's always wanted to visit, but the excursion takes an unexpected turn when they encounter something ancient and dangerous.

Not all family outings go as planned

Arrival in Adrestia

After the forest incident, Lydia finds herself in a place unlike any she's known - Adrestia, a hidden sanctuary where she meets Alex and Felix who introduce her to a world beyond her imagination.

A new world reveals itself
PART TWO: Discovery

Learning the Truth

Lydia learns about the Galaxian universe, the thirty-two planets, and the system of magic called "tongues." She discovers she's not who she thought she was.

Identity is more complex than it appears

The Council Meeting Plan

Alex, Felix, and Lydia learn about an important Galaxian Council meeting happening on Ludor. They decide to sneak away from Adrestia to attend and learn what the Councillors are discussing.

Some meetings are too important to miss

Journey to Ludor

The trio takes the Aegis spaceship to travel to Ludor, navigating challenges along the way as they make their first interplanetary journey.

Space travel isn't as smooth as it seems

Galaxian Council

They manage to infiltrate the Council meeting and overhear discussions about important discoveries, including mention of the Zureyan Tablets.

Eavesdropping on the most powerful comes with risks
PART THREE: Consequences

Unexpected Detour

After the Council meeting, the trio faces complications that force them to make an unplanned stop on a different planet, extending their journey beyond what they anticipated.

Not all destinations are planned

Seeking Help

To continue their journey home, they need to repair their ship, which leads them to seek assistance from an unexpected source with the skills they need.

Sometimes help comes from unexpected places

Unraveling Mysteries

As they work on their return journey, Lydia begins to piece together clues about her past and the true nature of events that have shaped her life.

The past is never truly forgotten

Preparing for What's Next

With new revelations and tools at their disposal, the trio prepares for the next phase of their journey, aware that greater challenges likely await them.

Every ending is a new beginning
