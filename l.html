About the Author

This book was written by a young author who discovered their passion for writing through the encouragement of an English teacher and supportive parents. The author began writing at a young age, publishing a poem in 'Once Upon a Dream' and even writing songs as early as age four.

The author's journey reflects how teachers can change lives and how small beginnings can lead to significant achievements, much like the quote from Percy Jackson they cherish: "even the small tides can make big tsunamis."

About the Story

Galaxian Chronicles follows the adventures of Lydia Nightshade, Alex Gardener, and Felix Darth as they navigate a universe of magical planets, ancient prophecies, and cosmic dangers.

The story explores themes of identity, friendship, and destiny across multiple worlds, featuring the mysterious Zureyan Tablets, the Galaxian Council, and the eternal struggle between cosmic forces.
