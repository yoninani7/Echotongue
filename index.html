About the Book

Discover the epic tale that spans across planets, blending fantasy and science fiction in a unique universe.

A Journey Across Galaxies

ECHOTONGUE: The Zureyan Tablets follows the story of Lydia Nightshade, a girl who discovers she's not who she thought she was. After a mysterious incident at her school, she finds herself in Adrestia, a hidden "planet" on Earth, where she learns about her true origins and the ancient magic that flows through the universe.

Joined by Alex Gardener and Felix Darth, Lydia embarks on a dangerous journey across planets to uncover the truth about the mysterious Zureyan Tablets and her own identity, while evading powerful forces that seek to control the ancient magic.

Inside the World

The story unfolds across 200+ pages of immersive storytelling, featuring intricate world-building with detailed magical systems, diverse planetary cultures, and remarkable character relationships.

Readers will journey through Earth, Adrestia, Ludor, Aster, and beyond, discovering ancient prophecies, corrupt councils, and the mysterious power of Echotongue itself.

Magic-Infused Dialects

In the Echotongue universe, language is power. Each planet has developed its own dialect infused with magical properties. Those who can master these languages can manipulate reality, control elements, and even alter minds.

Lydia Nightshade possesses a rare gift: the ability to speak all thirty-two dialects. This makes her both invaluable and dangerous to the powers that rule the universe.

About the Author

S.E. Blackwood is an award-winning speculative fiction author known for intricate world-building and complex characters. With a background in linguistics and mythology, Blackwood creates universes where language itself becomes a source of power and danger.

Echotongue is the culmination of seven years of research and writing, drawing inspiration from diverse mythologies, language structures, and the eternal human quest for truth in the face of overwhelming power.
