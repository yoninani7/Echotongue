Echotongue: The Zureyan Tablets - Chapter 1

Space has always been kind of magical to me. There's something comforting about how quiet it is up there, no arguments, no chores, no pretending. The moon especially - I loved how it gave off this soft white glow, like it was trying to be the sun's gentler cousin.

I'm Lydia Nightshade. My parents died when I was a baby... at least, that's what Aunt Silena always said. A plane crash, she claimed. But that never felt true. I don't remember much, just faint images, blurry faces with kind eyes leaning over me. Faces that didn't match the story she told. Silena acted like they had never existed. Like I came from nowhere and belonged to no one.

It's hard to describe her. She wasn't cruel in the obvious, movie-villain way. She was more like a rulebook come to life. Everything had to be perfect: my clothes, my posture, my behavior. At home, she was cold and bossy, but once we stepped outside, she turned sugary sweet. Smiles, compliments, polite small talk... total fakery. People thought she was lovely. They didn't see the version I lived with.

About the Book

Discover the epic tale that spans across planets, blending fantasy and science fiction in a unique universe.

A Journey Across Galaxies

ECHOTONGUE: The Zureyan Tablets follows the story of Lydia Nightshade, a girl who discovers she's not who she thought she was. After a mysterious incident at her school, she finds herself in Adrestia, a hidden "planet" on Earth, where she learns about her true origins and the ancient magic that flows through the universe.

Joined by Alex Gardener and Felix Darth, Lydia embarks on a dangerous journey across planets to uncover the truth about the mysterious Zureyan Tablets and her own identity, while evading powerful forces that seek to control the ancient magic.

What awaits you in echotongue

Explore the unique elements of this interplanetary universe

Adrestia

The only "planet" in the Universe that exists on Earth itself, hidden from mortal eyes. A sanctuary for Galaxians and a training ground for young heroes.

Tongues

Ancient languages that channel magic. Each planet has its own tongue, with words that can manipulate elements, create spells, and unlock hidden powers.

Galaxian Council

The governing body of the known Universe, consisting of eight Councillors who oversee the thirty-two planets and maintain order across galaxies.

Zureyan Tablets

Ancient stone tablets that hold the secrets of the Universe's past, present, and future. Their discovery could change the balance of power across all planets.

Galaxian Creatures

Magical beings from across the planets, from Pegasus that can fly through space to dangerous creatures like the Taurconis that require specific methods to defeat.

Interplanetary Travel

Advanced spacecraft that can travel between planets in minutes, not years. The Aegis is one such ship that plays a crucial role in the story.

The Galaxian Universe

Explore the key worlds of the Echotongue universe. Each planet has its own unique environment, culture, and magical dialect. The story spans across multiple celestial realms in a universe of thirty-two planets.

Inside the World

The book follows the story of Lydia Nightshade, a girl who discovers she's not who she thought she was. After a mysterious incident at her school, she finds herself in Adrestia, a hidden "planet" on Earth, where she learns about her true origins and the ancient magic that flows through the universe.

Joined by Alex Gardener and Felix Darth, Lydia embarks on a dangerous journey across planets to uncover the truth about the mysterious Zureyan Tablets and her own identity, while evading powerful forces that seek to control the ancient magic.

The story unfolds across 200+ pages of absorbing storytelling, featuring intricate world-building with detailed magical systems, diverse planetary cultures, and remarkable character relationships.

Readers will journey through Earth, Adrestia, Ludor, Aster, and beyond, discovering ancient prophecies, corrupt councils, and the mysterious power of Echotongue itself.

Magic-Infused Dialects

In the Echotongue universe, language is literally power. Each of the thirty-two planets has developed its own "tongue" - a dialect infused with magical properties. Those who can master these languages can manipulate reality, control elements, and perform incredible feats.

Lydia Nightshade possesses a rare gift: the ability to instinctively use multiple tongues, including the legendary Echotongue - a language powerful enough to challenge the Beyond itself. This makes her both invaluable and dangerous to the powers that rule the universe.

About the Author

Hermona Zeleke is a remarkable young author who began her writing journey at an early age. Her passion for storytelling was ignited by her English teacher in primary school, who recognized her talent and encouraged her to pursue writing.

Before this book, she had already published poetry in an anthology called 'Once Upon a Dream' and experimented with songwriting from the age of four. Her diverse creative background brings a unique voice to the fantasy genre.

She has also wrote the draft of ECHOTONGUE in under three months, balancing her schoolwork with her passion for storytelling. The book represents her fascination with cosmic mysteries, ancient legends, and the journey of self-discovery.

Hermona credits her parents and teachers for supporting her creative journey and hopes her story will inspire other young writers to pursue their dreams.

"Even the small tides can make big tsunamis." - A favorite quote from Percy Jackson that inspires Hermona's writing philosophy.
